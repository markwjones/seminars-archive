Computer Science

Departmental Colloquia Archive (1993-2011) plus other talks

The Departmental Colloquium series was organised by Computer Science with talks given by invited speakers. Further talks were given as part of the Proof, Complexity and Verification series and Algebraic Specification series which were placed online using this system until 2017. All subsequent talks moved to the University CMS and these talks are stored as is in this archive (external links may be broken).
